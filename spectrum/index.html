Spectrum

A band of colors, as seen in a rainbow, produced by the addition of the components of red, green and blue light according to wavelength.

A band of colors, as seen in a rainbow, produced by the addition of the components of red, green and blue light according to wavelength.

Designed to provide a better balance than the rainbow for warm colors and with a selection closer to a color wheel. Useful in saving hours, days and weeks of time in the early stages of a project. No theory, just color for now.

I use Spectrum when designing to quickly narrow down color choices. It is also useful in seeing how colors perform across different devices.
