Heart

This is my heart. There are many like it, but this one is mine. My heart is my best friend. It is my life. I must master it as I must master my life. My heart, without me, is useless. Without my heart, I am useless. I must design my heart true. I must protect my heart from the mindless dabbling of those with little or no understanding of visual logic. I will…

My heart and myself know that what counts is not just the visual. We know that it is distinctiveness, visibility, adaptability, memorability, universality and timelessness that count. I will remember this…

My heart is human, even as I, because it is my life. Thus, I will learn it as my hand. I will learn its weaknesses and its strength. I will ever guard it against the ravages of bad design. I will keep my heart refined without disturbing its identity. We will become part of each other. We will…

Before the Internets, I swear this creed. My heart and myself are the defenders of good design. We are the masters of our identities.

So be it, until there is no bad design!

Defend good design. Draw the heart. Be surprised with the time you’ll spend and things you’ll learned with such a simple exercise.
