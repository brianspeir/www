Brian Speir

Brian Speir is a designer in California.

I am a designer. I am a problem solver. I am a maker. Pixels are my paintbrush. Whiteboards expand my mind. Code is a tool in my toolbox.

When it comes to creative influence, I can never ignore where I grew up. I loved the many years I spent at Apple. But that's all I'm allowed to say. I am forever focused on surprising and delighting people with a unique blend of design and technology, that is my calling… It is who I am.

Elsewhere on the Internets you can follow me, connect with me, or fork the things I build. But if you really want to get to know me, let's do espresso.
