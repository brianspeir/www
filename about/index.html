I believe that good design comes from a consistent, honest design language that gets out of the way and emphasizes the usefulness of a product, thereby empowering self-expression and enjoyment.

When it comes to creative direction, I can never ignore where I grew up or the time spent building houses and cars with my father. I loved the many years I worked at Apple. I must warn you of my unwavering optimism, as I am forever focused on surprising and delighting people.

I am a devoted husband and dedicated handyman. When not in front of a screen you can find me behind a camera or tearing something apart and putting it back together again. I might even be obsessing over which paper to use for my next letter or card right now… yes, I still use and love paper! These are who I am.
