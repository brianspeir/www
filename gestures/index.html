Touch and Hold

Designed for use in wireframes with an emphasis on their ability to convey action without animation. Reflecting the ideal 44 pixel touch point and applied with a 50 percent transparency to allow visibility of the underlying element. Set in grey to avoid conflict when color is used to guide a users interaction. Most effective on their own layer to allow ultimate flexibility in generating collateral.
