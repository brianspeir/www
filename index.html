About Brian Speir who is a designer and maker in California.

I am a designer and a maker. Sometimes I push pixels. Sometimes I push code. When it comes to creative influence, I can never ignore where I grew up. I've built startups and failed. I've built others and sold them. There are many things I'll never be able to share about my years at Apple, except that I loved every one of them. I am forever focused on surprising and delighting people with a unique blend of design and technology, that is my calling… It is who I am.

Elsewhere on the Internets you can follow me, connect with me, or fork the things I build. But if you really want to get to know me, buy me an espresso.
